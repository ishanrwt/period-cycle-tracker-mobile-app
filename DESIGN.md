# Serene Clinical Design System

## Brand & Style
The design system is anchored in a philosophy of **Clinical Sophistication**. It balances the rigor of a medical-grade diagnostic tool with the warmth of a personal wellness companion. 

## Colors (Hex Codes)
- **Background**: `#FDFBF7`
- **Primary / Typography**: Dark Charcoal (`#333333`)
- **Secondary / Metadata**: Muted Gray (`#888888`)

### Phase Colors
- **Menstruation**: Dusty Pink (`#E8B4B8`)
- **Follicular**: Powder Blue (`#B8CBD0`)
- **Ovulation**: Sage Green (`#A8B5A2`)
- **Luteal**: Warm Taupe (`#D8C9B0`)
- **Phase Card Backgrounds**:
  - Menstruation: `#E8B4B8` at 10% opacity
  - Follicular: `#B8CBD0` at 10% opacity
  - Ovulation (Active): `#A8B5A2` at 20% opacity (with 30% border)
  - Luteal: `#D8C9B0` at 10% opacity

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter
- **Display**: 40px, Semi-Bold (600), Line Height: 48px, Tracking: -0.02em
- **Headline (Large)**: 32px, Semi-Bold (600), Line Height: 40px, Tracking: -0.02em
- **Headline (Medium)**: 20px, Medium (500), Line Height: 28px
- **Body (Large)**: 18px, Regular (400), Line Height: 28px
- **Body (Medium)**: 16px, Regular (400), Line Height: 24px
- **Label (Medium)**: 14px, Medium (500), Line Height: 20px, Tracking: 0.01em
- **Label (Small)**: 12px, Semi-Bold (600), Line Height: 16px, Tracking: 0.04em

## Layout & Spacing
- **Unit**: 4px
- **Stack Small**: 8px
- **Stack Medium**: 16px
- **Stack Large**: 32px
- **Mobile Margin**: 20px

## Shapes & Radii
- **Cards**: `rounded-2xl` (16px) or `rounded-xl` (24px)
- **Buttons / Ring**: `rounded-full` (9999px)

## Components
- **Progress Ring**: 240x240px container. Conic gradient spanning the 4 phases. Inner circle inset 24px. Indicator dot (Day) 16x16px positioned along ring.
- **Log Period Button**: Solid fill `#333333`, text `#FFFFFF`. Height: `56px`. Full pill-shaped rounding.
- **Phase Forecast Cards**: Horizontal layout. 12x12px (`w-3 h-3`) indicator dot. Outer padding 16px (`p-4`). Soft backdrop-blur and 2px shadow.
